**Note on SQLite and `beforeConnect`:** The SQLite dialect's `ConnectionManager` overrides `getConnection()` directly, bypassing the connection pool and its `_connect()` method where `beforeConnect` hooks are normally triggered. To ensure the hook executes, `sequelize.runHooks('beforeConnect', sequelize.config)` is called explicitly before `sequelize.authenticate()`. This preserves the hook's purpose — dynamically mutating the config — while working within SQLite's connection management constraints.
